No Curfew

UI/UX Branding

Everything You Need to Stay Up All Night

Originated from the idea of creating a competitor to GoPuff, No Curfew is a late-night delivery service that brings snacks, alcohol, and household items to your doorstep. This was developed as an intern project during my time at Box 8 Creative with fellow intern Alex Delfini.

Role

Co-Designer

Deliverables

Studio

Box 8 Creative; Intern Project

No Curfew is the “cool older sister” of the industry. With a lifestyle-focused narrative, it emphasizes that the people; complete with their 1 a.m. laughs, movie nights, and post-games to remember, are the most important part of the brand experience. No Curfew aims to celebrate the spirit of togetherness and not only connect with consumers beyond the delivery purchase, but also help them connect with each other.

App Design

We aimed to create an intuitive app that supports No Curfews’ elevated feeling and lifestyle-centered visuals while still ensuring a clean and accessible digital experience. A key focus was to avoid the overwhelming clutter seen on our competitors’ apps by creating a simple layout and streamlined information architecture.

In-Order Conversation Cards

Thought-provoking conversation cards will come in the bag of every No Curfew order. These aim to integrate the brand into consumers' lifestyles and emphasize values of connection and community.

Social

Due to the primary college-aged target audience, No Curfew's Instagram presence is a key part of the brand's social strategy, focusing on lifestyle imagery and simple graphics with open-ended questions.
