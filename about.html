About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary designer with passion for visual, UI/UX, web, and product design. Through carefully crafted narratives and purposeful, research-backed visual communication, I strive to create immersive experiences and compelling work that makes a difference. Accessibility and equality are core to my values, which are reflected in my work through themes of inclusivity and a focus on best accessibility practices.

When I'm not designing, you can find me trying new restaurants, binge-watching Netflix- I'm obsessed with the show Sex and the City lately (a classic, I know), and playing with any cat I see.
