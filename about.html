About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary designer with a passion for branding and UI/UX. Through carefully crafted narratives and purposeful, research-backed visual communication, I strive to create immersive experiences and compelling work that makes a difference. Accessibility and equality are core to my values, reflected in my work through themes of inclusivity and a focus on best accessibility practices.

When I'm not designing, you can find me trying new restaurants, thrifting, and playing with any cat I see.
