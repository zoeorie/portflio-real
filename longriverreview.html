Long River Review

Print

By Students, For Students

Long River Review is the University of Connecticut's annual literary magazine run by undergraduate students. I worked on a team of 5 graphic design students to curate and design the 2025 edition. After reading through all of the selected writing and choosing the artwork, we developed a brand that reflects the themes and subject matter of the issue.
