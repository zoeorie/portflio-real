Duck Pond Landscaping

LAWN CARE COMPANY

Ongoing marketing materials for Duck Pond Landscaping, Granby Connecticut's premier lawn care company. I was originally asked to create a business card for Duck Pond, which quickly grew into an all-encompassing brand campaign to elevate the local business.

Print Design Branding

Door Hangers

The objective was to create a clean, friendly design to advertise Duck Pond's services to potential new clients. I also focused on incorporating the branding elements created for the business card into the design to ensure cohesion. I collaborated with the client to develop a lighthearted concept for the door hangers centered around a positive customer review.

Social Media

The primary goal was to create effective, engaging content for Duck Pond's Facebook page to enhance the company's visibility online and increase user interaction with the page. I developed original content that focused on highlighting the company's work and providing concise information about lawncare and maintanace to showcase Duck Pond's expertise in the field.

Timeline

I created content and posted three times a week from May 2023-August 2023.

Impact

The total reach of the account increased from 2.6k to 5.5k within the timeline of my posts.

Website

For my most recent project with Duck Pond Landscaping, I was responsible for designing their first website. The client wanted a streamlined one-page site to effectively showcase their services, highlight photos of their best work, and provide a brief introduction to their business for the local community. To achieve this, I strategically incorporated buttons throughout the page, guiding visitors to an embedded form for easy access to free quotes. The design focused on a clean layout and intuitive navigation, catering specifically to Duck Pond's older customer base.
